Hound - Family Pet Care

The motivation for Hound started about four years ago. I had just transfered high schools and was looking for a way to continue my computer science education. I had already taken AP CS and my new high school didn't have a course difficult enough for me. Therefore, I proposed an independent study, where I continued my education by myself. In order to have something to work on, I proposed that I self-teach myself all the technologies required to built an iOS app. This app solve an issue our family was experiencing: our new puppy was still having accident because we had difficulty tracking who took her out last.

Functional requirements/what the project does In its current state, Hound has solved the issue I set out to remedy. Hound allows for a whole family to collaborate on their dogs care. It allows for them to create reminders to never forget to take care of their dog (potty, feed, walk...) Additionally, it has an intricate logging system to both track who took care of the dog and when, sending notifications to all family members when someone lends a helping hand to the dogs.

To be able to accomplish what it does, Hound has three primary components: - An iOS app - A Node.js server - A MariaDB database The app is user-facing, allowing for the viewing and input of data. The server manages all requests from the app, verifying identities, processing data, interacting with the database, and sending out notifications The database contains all of the user, family, and dog data, storing it in the cloud to allow for seamless synchronization between multipe devices
